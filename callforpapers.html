Call for Papers 2027

Present your research at V-CIM 2027 - the premier national forum for VLSI design, intelligent communication, and machine learning systems. Join us in hybrid mode at OUTR, Bhubaneswar from 7-9 January 2027.

Tracks & Topics of Interest

VLSI & Embedded Systems Low-power VLSI, FPGA/ASIC Design, Hardware Security, Approximate Computing, SoC, NoC
Intelligent Machines & AI Edge AI, ML Accelerators, Neuromorphic Computing, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Robotics
6G & Advanced Communication Massive MIMO, Cognitive Radio, THz Communications, Visible Light Comm., Optical Networks

Cybersecurity & VLSI Hardware Trojans, PUFs, Lightweight Encryption, Secure Communication Protocols

Submission Guidelines

Paper Format
Full papers (4-6 pages) in IEEE double-column format. Include abstract, keywords, figures, references.

Language
English only. Plagiarism less than 15% (Turnitin check).

Presentation
Hybrid mode: In-person at OUTR Bhubaneswar OR virtual via Zoom. Each accepted paper needs one full registration.

Best Paper Awards
Selected papers from each track will receive Best Paper Award certificates and cash prizes.

Note: All submissions must be original and not simultaneously submitted elsewhere. At least one author must register by early-bird deadline for inclusion in proceedings.
