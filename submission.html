Submission & Registration | V-CIM 2027

Authors are invited to submit original research papers in VLSI, Communication, Intelligent Machines, and related areas. All submissions undergo rigorous double-blind peer-review. At least one author per accepted paper must register (early-bird encouraged) and present for possible inclusion in IEEE Xplore. Fees are in INR + 18% GST.

Submit via CMT Register Online Submission: 15 Nov 2026 | Early Bird: 20 Dec 2026

Submission Guidelines

  • Full paper: 4-6 pages in IEEE double-column conference format
  • Short paper: Up to 3 pages (poster/presentation only)
  • Double-blind review: Remove author names/affiliations
  • Plagiarism limit: 15% maximum similarity (iThenticate)
  • Language: English only

At least one author must register and present (in-person/virtual) for possible inclusion in IEEE Xplore.

Review Process & Publication Ethics

All papers undergo similarity check followed by double-blind peer review (at least 2 reviewers). Conference follows COPE guidelines. By submitting, authors confirm originality and that work is not under consideration elsewhere.

Extra Page Charges

Papers exceeding 6 pages (maximum allowed: 8 pages) will incur extra charges:

  • Rs. 500 per extra page (for Indian authors)
  • Maximum two additional pages allowed.

Group Registration Benefits

For group registrations (3+ delegates from same institute): 10% discount on total registration fees. Please contact conference secretariat.

Important Notes & Policies

  • Mandatory Registration: At least one author per accepted paper must register under "Regular Author" category and present for possible inclusion in IEEE Xplore.
  • One registration covers only one paper (max 6 pages). Additional papers require separate co-author registration.
  • Registration includes: access to all technical sessions, conference kit (for in-person attendees), e-certificate, lunch & refreshments.
  • Cancellation Policy: No refunds for cancellation after registration completion. Before 31 December 2026, 50% refund (processing charges deducted).
  • Accommodation: Participants need to arrange their own stay. Limited on-campus guest house available on request.
  • All fees are in Indian Rupees (INR). 18% GST is additional as per government norms.
