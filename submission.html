Paper Submission Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit original, unpublished research papers addressing topics related to VLSI design, Communication Systems, Intelligent Machines, Signal Processing, and emerging areas. All submissions undergo rigorous double-blind peer-review.

Submit via CMT Submission Deadline: 15 November 2026

Submission Guidelines

  • Full paper: 4-8 pages (IEEE double-column format)
  • Short paper: Up to 3 pages (poster/presentation only)
  • Double-blind review: Remove author names/affiliations
  • Plagiarism limit: 15% maximum similarity (iThenticate)
  • Language: English only

At least one author must register and present (in-person/virtual) for inclusion in proceedings.

Review Process & Publication Ethics

All papers undergo similarity check followed by double-blind peer review (at least 2 reviewers). Conference follows COPE guidelines. By submitting, authors confirm originality and that work is not under consideration elsewhere.
