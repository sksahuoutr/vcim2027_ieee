Submission & Registration | V-CIM 2027

Authors are invited to submit original research papers in VLSI, Communication, Intelligent Machines, and related areas. All submissions are peer-reviewed in a double-blind process. At least one author of each accepted paper must register and present for possible inclusion in IEEE Xplore.

Submit via CMT Register Online Submission: 15 Nov 2026 | Early Bird: 20 Dec 2026

Submission Guidelines

  • Full paper: 4-6 pages in IEEE double-column conference format
  • Short paper: Up to 3 pages (poster/presentation only)
  • Double-blind review: Remove author names and affiliations
  • Plagiarism limit: 15% maximum similarity (iThenticate)
  • Language: English only

At least one author must register and present (in-person or virtual) for possible inclusion in IEEE Xplore.

Important Deadlines

Review Process & Publication Ethics

All papers undergo similarity check followed by double-blind peer review by at least two reviewers. The conference follows COPE guidelines. By submitting, authors confirm originality and that the work is not under review elsewhere.

  • Best Paper Awards: Certificates and cash prizes for top papers.
  • IEEE Publication: Accepted and presented papers will be submitted for possible inclusion in IEEE Xplore.

Extra Page Charges

Papers exceeding 6 pages (maximum allowed: 8 pages) will incur extra charges:

  • Rs. 500 per extra page (for Indian authors)
  • Maximum two additional pages are allowed.

Group Registration Benefits

For group registrations (3 or more delegates from the same institute): 10% discount on total registration fees. Please contact the conference secretariat.

Important Notes & Policies

  • Mandatory Registration: At least one author per accepted paper must register under the "Regular Author" category and present for possible inclusion in IEEE Xplore.
  • One registration covers only one paper (maximum 6 pages). Additional papers require separate co-author registration.
  • Registration includes access to sessions, conference kit (for in-person attendees), e-certificate, lunch and refreshments.
  • Cancellation Policy: No refunds for cancellation after registration completion. Before 31 December 2026, a 50% refund is available after processing deductions.
  • Accommodation: Participants need to arrange their own stay. Limited on-campus guest house rooms may be available on request.
  • All fees are in Indian Rupees (INR). 18% GST is additional as per government norms.
