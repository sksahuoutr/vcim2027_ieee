Publication & Indexing

IEEE conference publication process, camera-ready requirements, and IEEE Xplore submission information for V-CIM 2027

IEEE Conference Proceedings

Accepted and presented papers from V-CIM 2027 will be submitted for possible inclusion in IEEE Xplore, subject to IEEE scope, quality, plagiarism, and formatting requirements.

  • IEEE double-column conference paper format
  • IEEE copyright and camera-ready compliance required
  • Presentation by at least one registered author is mandatory
  • IEEE Xplore inclusion is subject to final IEEE acceptance

Indexing & Abstracting

IEEE Xplore records are discoverable through IEEE and indexing partner services according to IEEE policies. The conference does not guarantee indexing by any third-party database.

Publication Ethics & Plagiarism Policy

V-CIM follows Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE) guidelines. All submissions undergo similarity check via iThenticate. Plagiarism above 15% (excluding references) leads to immediate rejection.

  • No simultaneous submissions
  • Mandatory author declaration form
  • Double-blind peer review

Important for Authors

  • At least one author must register and present (in-person/virtual)
  • Final camera-ready paper must incorporate review comments
  • IEEE copyright agreement is mandatory
  • IEEE Xplore submission is subject to IEEE quality checks
