Extra Page Charges

Papers exceeding 6 pages (maximum allowed: 8 pages) will incur extra charges:

  • Rs. 500 per extra page (for Indian authors)
  • Maximum two additional pages allowed.

Group Registration Benefits

For group registrations (3+ delegates from same institute): 10% discount on total registration fees. Please contact conference secretariat.

Important Notes & Policies

  • Mandatory Registration: At least one author per accepted paper must register under "Regular Author" category for inclusion in IEEE proceedings.
  • One registration covers only one paper (max 6 pages). Additional papers require separate co-author registration.
  • Registration includes: access to all technical sessions, conference kit (for in-person attendees), e-certificate, lunch & refreshments.
  • Cancellation Policy: No refunds for cancellation after registration completion. Before 31 December 2026, 50% refund (processing charges deducted).
  • Accommodation: Participants need to arrange their own stay. Limited on-campus guest house available on request.
  • All fees are in Indian Rupees (INR) and inclusive of 18% GST as per government norms.
