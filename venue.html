Venue & Travel

V-CIM 2027 will be hosted at Odisha University of Technology and Research (OUTR), Bhubaneswar. The conference will operate in hybrid mode, enabling both in-person and virtual participation.

Venue

Odisha University of Technology and Research

Odisha University of Technology and Research (OUTR)
Chintamaniswari Area, Kalinga Nagar,
Bhubaneswar, Odisha - 751029, India

+91-674-2381234 | vcim2027@outr.ac.in

OUTR is a leading state university located in Bhubaneswar, with academic strengths across engineering, electronics, communication, computing, and interdisciplinary research. In-person sessions, registration desk, help desk, and author interaction areas will be hosted on campus.

In-Person

Technical sessions, keynotes, networking, and campus interactions at OUTR.

Virtual Mode

Approved online presentations and live participation through the conference platform.

Travel

By Air

Biju Patnaik International Airport (BBI) is about 8 km from OUTR. Taxi and app-based cab services are available from the airport.

Approx. cab fare: Rs.250-400.

By Railway

Bhubaneswar Railway Station (BBS) is about 6 km from OUTR and is well connected to major Indian cities.

Approx. cab/auto fare: Rs.150-250.

By Road

Bhubaneswar is connected through NH-16 and state transport routes. Baramunda bus terminal and city bus services provide local connectivity.

Local Commute

Ola, Uber, auto-rickshaws, local taxis, and Mo Bus services are available for commuting between hotels, venue, airport, and railway station.

Travel Tip: Delegates arriving on 6 January 2027 are advised to pre-book accommodation and airport/station transfers during peak conference hours.

Accommodation

Delegates may choose hotels near Jayadev Vihar, Khandagiri, Nayapalli, Patia, or the airport corridor depending on budget and travel preference. Limited university guest house accommodation may be available on request and subject to approval.

  • OUTR Guest House / University Accommodation Limited availability
  • Hotel Sandy's Tower / nearby budget hotels ~2-4 km
  • The Crown / business hotels near Nayapalli ~3-5 km
  • Mayfair Convention / premium hotels near Jayadev Vihar ~4-6 km
  • Airport-area hotels ~6-9 km
Participants are requested to arrange accommodation directly. The conference secretariat may share hotel suggestions and local guidance but bookings remain the participant's responsibility.

Weather Condition During Jan 2027 in Bhubaneswar

January in Bhubaneswar is generally mild, dry, and comfortable for travel. For the V-CIM 2027 conference dates, delegates can expect typical winter conditions, with warm afternoons and cooler mornings/evenings.

Temperature

Typical January range: around 16 C at night to 28-29 C during the day.

Sky & Rain

Mostly clear to partly cloudy. January is one of the driest months in Bhubaneswar.

What to Carry

Light cottons for daytime, a light jacket or sweater for early mornings and evenings.

For final V-CIM 2027 travel planning, delegates should check the local forecast again in late December 2026 or early January 2027.

Places of Tourist Interest in Bhubaneswar and Nearby

Bhubaneswar is known as the Temple City of India and is close to several cultural, heritage, wildlife, and coastal destinations. Delegates may plan short visits before or after the conference.

Lingaraj Temple

Major heritage temple in old Bhubaneswar, about 5-7 km from OUTR.

Udayagiri & Khandagiri Caves

Ancient Jain rock-cut caves and viewpoints, about 7 km from the venue.

Mukteswar & Rajarani Temples

Important examples of Odisha temple architecture in Bhubaneswar.

Nandankanan Zoological Park

Zoo, botanical garden, and safari attraction about 15-18 km from OUTR.

Dhauli Shanti Stupa

Peace pagoda and historic Kalinga war site, around 12-15 km from Bhubaneswar.

Puri & Konark

Jagannath Temple, Puri beach, and Konark Sun Temple are popular day-trip destinations.

Chilika Lake

Asia's largest brackish water lagoon, suitable for a longer post-conference excursion.

Ekamra Haat

Local handicrafts, handloom, and Odisha cuisine market within the city.
