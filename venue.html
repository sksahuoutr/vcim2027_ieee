Conference Venue

V-CIM 2027 will be held at the prestigious Odisha University of Technology and Research (OUTR), Bhubaneswar - formerly known as College of Engineering and Technology (CET). The conference will operate in HYBRID mode, allowing both in-person and virtual participation.

How to Reach Bhubaneswar

By Air

Biju Patnaik International Airport (BBI)
Located ~8 km from OUTR. Direct flights from Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Chennai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad.

Cab fare: ~Rs.250-350

By Railway

Bhubaneswar Railway Station (BBS)
~6 km from OUTR. Major rail hub connecting to all Indian cities.

Cab/auto fare: ~Rs.150-200

By Road

Baramunda Bus Stand / Master Canteen
Well-connected by OSRTC and private buses from all major Odisha cities and neighboring states.

Local Transport & Commute

Ola / Uber

Available 24/7 throughout Bhubaneswar. Approx fare from airport/railway station to OUTR: Rs.200-400.

Rentals / Auto

Mooved, Rentabike, local autos available. Auto from station: ~Rs.100-150.

Mo Bus (City Bus)

AC city bus service. Route #12, #25 stop near OUTR. Fare: Rs.20-50.

Explore Bhubaneswar - The Temple City

Lingaraj Temple

11th century ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. ~5 km from venue.

Udayagiri & Khandagiri Caves

Ancient Jain rock-cut caves with inscriptions. ~7 km from venue.

Nandankanan Zoo

Famous white tiger safari & botanical garden. ~15 km from venue.

Ekamra Haat

Handicrafts market & Odisha cuisine. ~4 km from venue.

January Weather in Bhubaneswar: Pleasant winter weather, 15 C - 28 C. Light woolens recommended for mornings/evenings.
